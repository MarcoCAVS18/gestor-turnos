1. Information We Collect
We collect information to provide better services to all our users.

1.1 Account Information

  • Authentication Data: Email address, display name, and profile photo (when using Google Sign-In or email registration).
  • Account Preferences: Language settings, theme preferences, notification settings, and other customization options.
  • Profile Information: Any additional information you choose to provide in your profile.

1.2 Work and Shift Data

  • Work Configurations: Job names, hourly rates, penalty rates (day/afternoon/night/weekend), and work descriptions.
  • Shift Records: Clock-in/clock-out times, break durations, shift dates, and associated work assignments.
  • Delivery Work Data: Platform information (e.g., DoorDash, Uber Eats), vehicle type, delivery earnings, tips, and mileage tracking.
  • Earnings Calculations: Computed earnings based on your configured rates (these are estimates only).
  • Tax and Deduction Settings: Tax rates, superannuation percentages, and custom deductions you configure.

1.3 Live Mode Data

  • Session Data: Start time, pause/resume events, total duration, and computed earnings during live tracking sessions.
  • Usage Metrics: Number of Live Mode sessions used per month (for free tier usage limits).

1.4 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type and version.
  • Connection Data: IP address, approximate location (country/region level only).
  • Usage Analytics: Feature usage patterns and error logs to improve the application.

2. Payment Processing & Stripe
Orary uses Stripe as our payment processor for Premium subscriptions.

2.1 Payment Data

When you subscribe to Orary Premium, payment is processed by Stripe's secure portal. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card number, CVV, or full bank account details.

2.2 Information We Receive from Stripe

  • Stripe Customer ID: A unique identifier linking your Orary account to Stripe.
  • Subscription Status: Whether your subscription is active, trialing, or cancelled.
  • Billing Period: Subscription start and end dates.
  • Last 4 Digits: Only the last 4 digits of your payment card (for display purposes).

2.3 Subscription Details

  • Trial: New users get a 15-day free trial. Your card is required but not charged until the trial ends.
  • Billing: $2.99 AUD/month after trial, charged automatically through Stripe.
  • Cancellation: Cancel at any time. Access continues until the end of the billing period.
  • Refunds: We do not provide refunds for partial billing periods.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Core Services: Enable shift tracking, earnings calculations, and data synchronization.
  • Sync Across Devices: Keep your data synchronized securely across all your devices.
  • Process Payments: Manage Premium subscriptions through Stripe.
  • Improve the Application: Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and develop new features.
  • Communicate with You: Send important updates about your account and security.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties
We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with trusted service providers.

5.1 Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database storage, and hosting.
  • Stripe: Payment processing for Premium subscriptions.

5.2 Work Sharing Feature

When you use the "Share Work" feature, a temporary share link is created. This data expires automatically after 7 days, has a maximum of 10 uses, and does not include your personal information or shift history.

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users.
